WHO WE ARE

rCUBE'S MATE IS THE DEDICATED PARTNERSHIP WEBSITE FOR THE rCUBE APP, DESIGNED SPECIFICALLY TO SUPPORT AND COLLABORATE WITH RECYCLING COMPANIES. THROUGH RCUBE APP, RCUBE'S MATE WILL TRACK THE RECYCLED ITEMS FROM THE USER ALONG WITH THE COMPLETION DETAILS OF THE RECYCLING PROCESS FOR THESE ITEMS.

OUR GOALS

rCUBE and rCUBE’s Mate are steadfast in their commitment to supporting Singapore’s 30 by 30 plan, aiming to enhance the nation's sustainability and self-sufficiency. Through our innovative recycling platform, we facilitate the efficient tracking and processing of recycled materials, thereby contributing to the reduction of waste and promotion of environmental stewardship.

AND

OUR MISSIONS

By collaborating closely with recycling companies, rCUBE’s Mate provides detailed data on recycling activities, enabling stakeholders to make informed decisions that align with the goals of the 30 by 30 plan. Our platform not only empowers users to participate actively in recycling efforts but also ensures that these efforts are meticulously documented and reported. Additionally, the rCUBE app rewards users with points based on the size and number of items recycled, incentivizing greater participation and commitment to sustainable practices.
